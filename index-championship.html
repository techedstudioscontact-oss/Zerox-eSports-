About Zerox eSports

Zerox eSports is India's premier professional gaming organization, dedicated to fostering competitive excellence and sportsmanship in the esports community. Founded in 2026, we organize world-class tournaments, develop professional players, and promote fair play across all gaming platforms.

Our mission is to elevate Indian esports to the global stage by providing professional infrastructure, transparent competition, and career opportunities for talented gamers. We believe in creating a sustainable ecosystem where champions are born and legends are made.

Rules & Fair Play

Our commitment to integrity and competitive excellence

Fair Play

Zero tolerance for cheating, hacking, or any form of unfair advantage. All players must compete with integrity.

Eligibility

Players must meet age requirements and provide valid identification. Team rosters must be submitted before deadlines.

Punctuality

All matches start on time. Teams must check in 30 minutes before scheduled match time or face disqualification.

Sportsmanship

Respect all players, officials, and staff. Toxic behavior, harassment, or abuse will not be tolerated.

Disputes

All disputes must be reported immediately to tournament officials. Decisions of referees are final.

Prize Distribution

Prizes awarded within 30 days of tournament conclusion. Winners must provide banking details and tax information.
