Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zerox eSports. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you register for our tournaments, purchase tickets, or visit our website.

By using our website and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for tournaments or purchase tickets, we collect:

  • Name: Full name and in-game name (IGN)
  • Contact Details: Email address and phone number
  • Location: City, state, and district information
  • Age: For eligibility verification
  • Game Information: UID (User ID), team name, game selection
  • Device Information: Phone brand and RAM specifications

2.2 Payment Information

When you make payments for tournament registration or ticket purchases, payment details are processed through secure third-party payment processors. We do not store complete payment card information on our servers.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referral source and navigation patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Tournament Management: Registration, team verification, bracket creation, and event logistics
  • Communication: Sending confirmation emails, venue details, payment instructions, and tournament updates
  • Verification: Age verification, device specification validation, and eligibility checks
  • Ticketing: Processing spectator and VIP pass purchases, sending e-tickets
  • Live Streaming: Displaying player names, team names, and in-game statistics during broadcasts
  • Marketing: Promotional emails about future tournaments (you can opt-out anytime)
  • Analytics: Understanding user behavior to improve our services

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Zerox eSports does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 When We Share Information

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Formspree, payment gateways), email services, hosting providers
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court orders, or government authorities
  • Public Display: Player names and team names on tournament brackets, leaderboards, and live streams (with your consent via registration)
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates

⚠️ Important: While we use reasonable security measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please use caution when sharing sensitive information online.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy
  • Comply with legal obligations and tournament records
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

Tournament Data: Stored for up to 3 years for historical records and statistics.
Marketing Data: Retained until you opt-out or request deletion.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@zeroxesports.com

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. For detailed information, please see our Cookie Policy.

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (social media, sponsors, partners). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

10. Children's Privacy

Our tournaments have minimum age requirements (16+ for players, 8+ for spectators). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 8 years old without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or website notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
