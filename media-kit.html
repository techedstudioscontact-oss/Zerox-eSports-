About Zerox eSports

Zerox eSports is India's premier professional gaming organization, dedicated to dominating the competitive esports scene. We specialize in BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) and Free Fire MAX tournaments, bringing together the best players across the nation.

Our mission is to elevate Indian esports, provide world-class tournament experiences, and create opportunities for talented gamers to showcase their skills on professional platforms.

Founded: 2026
Headquarters: India
First Tournament: Hazaribagh, Jharkhand (October 12-14, 2026)
Games: BGMI, Free Fire MAX
